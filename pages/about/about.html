Leading with Purpose: Our Founder's Insight

The vision and values behind two decades of environmental stewardship.

Amit Gandhe

Founder, SIEC

~20 years

Leading environmental projects

Sustainability shouldn't stay a bold idea on paper — it should become a lived, working reality for every client and community we serve.

Amit Gandhe founded SIEC after nearly two decades leading environmental and infrastructure projects across Singapore, the Middle East, and beyond. His background spans infrastructure engineering, business development, project management, and engineering leadership, having steered multimillion-dollar water infrastructure programs for communities in diverse regions worldwide.

Having held senior roles across both EPCM and consultancy — including leading smart city and urban infrastructure initiatives for major emerging cities — he shaped SIEC around a single idea: consultancies often hand over brilliant, forward-thinking sustainability plans that stall against budgets, competing priorities, and organizational inertia. SIEC exists to carry those ideas the rest of the way, turning ambitious environmental thinking into projects that actually get built.
